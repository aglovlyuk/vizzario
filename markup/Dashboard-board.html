Vision performance index
Definition

Vision Perfromance Index (VPI) represents a summary of your overall vision performance: FOV, ACC, MULT, END, DET.

Application

The goals of VPI are to make employees more aware of their vision, monitor their vision performance over time and give them tips/recommendations to help maintain vision at a high level.
